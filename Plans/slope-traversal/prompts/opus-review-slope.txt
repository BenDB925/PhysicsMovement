# Plan 09 — Slope Traversal

The character currently struggles on inclines — feet don't plant correctly on slopes, height maintenance fights the terrain, and uphill movement is slow and jank.

Three stacked problems:
1. Foot rotation ignores surface normal — feet stay world-aligned (flat) on slopes
2. Height maintenance targets world Y — hips fight the slope as it rises, draining speed
3. Move force is horizontal — ~82% effective uphill component on 35° slope

Existing infrastructure: surfaceNormalQuality concept in StepPlanner/FootContactObservation; ApplyStepUpFootLiftAssist in BalanceController.

Proposed slices:
1. Slope detection seam — BalanceController raycast, expose SurfaceNormal / SlopeAngleDeg / SurfaceNormalQuality, debug log
2. Surface-relative height maintenance — targetHipsY = hitPoint.y + _standingHipsHeight, gated on slope < 50° and quality > 0.5
3. Foot rotation on slope — LegAnimator foot IK target blends toward surface normal at landing position
4. Forward lean on slope — BalanceController uprightTarget tilt, visual only, gated on uphill movement
5. Tests — SlopeTraversalTests.cs: time to top of 35° 8m incline, foot alignment angle, hips height variance, flat ground regression
